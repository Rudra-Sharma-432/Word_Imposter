🥷 Word Imposter

ABOUT THE GAME

Word Imposter is a social deduction party game where players try to identify the hidden Imposter among them. Everyone receives a secret word — except one player. The Imposter must blend in during the discussion and avoid being exposed.

👥 3–10 Players 🎲 Party Game 🕵 Social Deduction

HOW TO PLAY

1

Join a Room

One player creates a room and shares the Room ID with friends. Others can join from the public list or enter a room code directly.

2

Roles Are Assigned

When the game starts, every player receives the same secret word — except one. That player secretly becomes the Imposter and receives no word.

3

Discussion Round

Players take turns describing the word without saying it directly. The Imposter must listen carefully and bluff like they know the word.

4

Vote!

After all players have spoken, everyone votes for who they think is the Imposter. If the Imposter survives the vote — they win!

💡 TIPS

Be descriptive but don't give the word away — subtlety wins.

Pay close attention to players who seem vague or overly generic.

If you're the Imposter, listen to others first to pick up clues.
